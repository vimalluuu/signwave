Privacy Policy for Sign Wave

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to Sign Wave! Your privacy is incredibly important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

Sign Wave is designed to respect your privacy by default.

  • Video & Camera Data: We do not store, record, or transmit your camera feed. All sign language translation processing is either handled locally on your device or sent securely for immediate, anonymous processing without being saved.
  • Personal Data: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our core translation features.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any minimal data collected—such as anonymous, standard web server logs (e.g., browser type, timestamps)—is used exclusively to:

  • Maintain and improve the performance of our website.
  • Secure our platform against unauthorized access or abuse.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use essential third-party services for web hosting or basic analytics to understand how our site is used. These third parties only have access to anonymous usage data and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
